FAQs

Common questions

Quick answers to billing, troubleshooting, and account requests. Full details live inside the ticket autoresponse and Terms of Service.

When is billing due?

Billing cycles follow your activation date. Reminders go out via SMS and email 5 days before due date.

How do I pay?

Pay via billing.fiberflow.com.ph using Xendit—GCash, Maya, cards, or OTC partners. Keep your account number ready.

How should I run a speed test?

Use a wired LAN connection direct to the ONU. Disconnect VPNs and pause heavy downloads for the most accurate result.

Gaming and CGNAT?

Residential plans use CGNAT. Request for dedicated IP options via ticket so we can propose alternatives.

Relocation or early disconnect?

No lock-in. For disconnect within 24 paid months, allow FiberFlow to retrieve the ONU and drop within 7 days.

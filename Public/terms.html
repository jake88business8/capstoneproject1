Terms of Service

Month-to-month with honest policies

FiberFlow delivers fiber internet on a month-to-month basis. These terms summarize key commitments and responsibilities for both FiberFlow and subscribers.

Effective: 21 Oct 2025 Jurisdiction: Romblon Province, Philippines

Service commitment

  • No lock-in. Service is month-to-month and cancelable anytime.
  • Unlimited data on a fairly managed network to protect quality for all users.
  • Residential plans operate behind CGNAT—no inbound public IP. Contact us for enterprise IP options.

Equipment ownership & retrieval

  • ONU and installation accessories remain FiberFlow property for 24 consecutive paid months.
  • After 24 months, ONU ownership transfers automatically to the subscriber.
  • If service ends before 24 months, subscribers provide access for retrieval within seven (7) days.
  • Lost or unintentionally damaged ONU: ₱0 charge. Non-return or denied access: ₱1,000 flat fee.
  • Outside plant accessories (drop, anchors, clamps) remain FiberFlow property unless explicitly transferred in writing.

Installation services

Installation is provided by independent FiberFlow-accredited installers. FiberFlow may collect installation charges on their behalf and issue an Acknowledgment Receipt, which is not evidence of sale. Installers issue the Sales Invoice covering installation amounts.

Payments & billing

  • Monthly service is invoiced by FiberFlow with VAT-inclusive pricing.
  • Pay via billing.fiberflow.com.ph using Xendit-supported channels (GCash, Maya, cards, OTC).
  • Billing notifications and dunning SMS follow our support FAQs and SMS/OTP policy.

Privacy

We process personal data for service activation, billing, and support consistent with our Privacy Policy.

FiberFlow Technologies Inc.

Romblon-based ISP headquartered at Sitio Tabing Dagat, Panique, Odiongan. We operate Panique Data Center and light Romblon’s only submarine cable with Infinivan.

Mission

Reliable, honest connectivity for every household. Build. Inspire.

We believe fiber internet should be transparent—clear pricing, no lock-in, and local faces answering every ticket.

Infinivan partnership

FiberFlow draws exclusive transport from Infinivan Inc. via the Calatrava submarine cable landing station, delivering a 100 Gbps backbone to Panique DC.

This capacity keeps peak-hour speeds steady as we grow San Andres, Odiongan, and beyond.

Panique Data Center

Odiongan’s neutral hub for power, cooling, and secure fiber distribution across Tablas Island.

Local operations

Our planners, installers, and NOC live in Romblon. We train local talent and reinvest in the province.

Community-first

We sponsor digital literacy, e-sports events, and emergency connectivity for public facilities.

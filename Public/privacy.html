Privacy Policy

Protecting your data under PH DPA 2012

FiberFlow Technologies Inc. complies with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 and its IRR. This notice explains what we collect, why, how we share it, how long we keep it, and your rights.

What we collect

  • Name and contact details (mobile, email, Messenger link).
  • Service addresses, installation coordinates, and drop length estimates.
  • Billing history, payment confirmations, and account numbers.
  • Network diagnostics (speed tests, modem logs) for support.

Why we process data

  • Service delivery: coverage validation, installation, activation.
  • Billing and payment processing.
  • Support, troubleshooting, and outage communication.
  • Fraud prevention, network security, and service improvement.

Sharing

We share data with authorized processors strictly for service fulfillment:

  • Billing and payment providers (PHPNuxBill, Xendit).
  • SMS, email, and Messenger platforms for notifications.
  • Accredited installers to complete site surveys and installs.

Retention

We retain personal data as required by law, regulatory obligations, and operational needs. Once no longer necessary, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

Your rights

  • Access, correction, and deletion (subject to service limitations).
  • Data portability where technically feasible.
  • Withdrawal of consent (may impact service delivery).
  • File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission.

Contact

Email privacy@fiberflow.com.ph for privacy questions or to exercise your rights.
